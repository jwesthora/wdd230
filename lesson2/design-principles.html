Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Apple has a great webpage that shows contrast with dark colors in the back and bright colors ontop of the black to make them pop out. As you scroll they use this concept in reverse as well, with light colors in the back and dark on top. Because of the contrast of dark and light colors, they end up drawing the eye to particular elements of the page that pop out the most.

Repetition

Recipe Runner

RecipeRunner.com

The Recipe Runner is a good example of repetative design with pictures upon pictures and text above and below. Many of the things on this sight have the same design with the same design throughout the page for displaying recipies.

Proximity

USA Furniture And Leather

USAFurnitureAndLeather.com

The proximity in the web page of USA Furniture and Leather show proximity within the photo of the bedroom set. Eventhought the words are not overlapping, they seem a little busy to be in one area. However, it is nice that the prximity seems to be broken up with the different styles of font and size. I think this shows proximity and how it can work when done right.
