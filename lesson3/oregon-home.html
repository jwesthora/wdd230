Purpose

We offer information on local parks, trails, events, and products offered by small businesses that are local to the residents and visitors of Portland Oregon, in an effort to support local park and recreation centers and local businesses. We offer connectivity between local businesses and the community park and recreation facilities to connect and collaborate with one another. We provide our audience with a variety of local options for entertainment, nature walks, park locations, home goods, food, and shopping to anyone that is visiting or wants to venture around Portland Oregon.
