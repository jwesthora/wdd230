Purpose

The purpose of this website is to bring fresh fruit drinks to those in Encinitas California. Provide some exciting ideas and photos for people to visit while in Encinitas California. Orders can be made on-line, and specified to order.

Audience

Our audience doesn't have an age requirement or limit, we are for the young and wise. We are targeting to attract more teens, by incorprating social media tags that can be shared and tagged. We are targeting families and individuals looking for something fresh and local.
